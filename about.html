  • Background

    Midland Automation Technology Ltd (MATL) was formed in 1993 as a limited company with many years of electrical, controls and automation experience in the industries of water and wastewater, biomass handling, energy from waste, chemicals, pharmaceutical, packaging and food. We also have resource available to develop mechanical designs and drawings and 3D CAD.
    Our aim is to be professional, approachable and flexible. We build up strong and long-lasting relationships with clients, but provide the level of confidentiality that should be expected from our type of business.
    Our services can provide either a comprehensive support to supplement in-house resources at a range of levels and at various points in the project cycle, or a complete design solution from initial concept through to construction completion and beyond.
    Regardless of the role we play in a project, we are always keenly aware of the need to manage cost, time and quality.
