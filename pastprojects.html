Past Projects

ITI Energy, Sheffield

MATL were engaged by ITI Energy initially to carry out compliance and validation of design documentation for a downdraught gasification plant under construction at the former Bentinck Colliery in Nottinghamshire, UK. Our level of expertise and opportunity led us to become the lead electrical engineering and design partner for the project from the process design stagees through construction support and plant commissioning, and assisted with achieving new innovations.

Biomass Handling - Graham Construction, Immingham and Liverpool Dock

MATL were selected by Graham Construction to reinforce their internal EC&I design capabilities on a major biomass pellet handling and storage plant on Humberside. The plant consisted of a ship offloading units, eight 50,000 tonne holding silos, two rail loading systems and a network of mass conveyor systems to transfer pellets into and out of the storage silos to the rail loading systems for onward transist to the power stations. MATL were closely involved with the definition of the automation and safety functions, risk management, logic control and third party interfaces.
